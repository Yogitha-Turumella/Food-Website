Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Our average delivery time is approximately 30-45 minutes, but it may vary depending on factors like order volume and distance. Rest assured, we're working hard to get your delicious meal to you as quickly as possible!

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS. Click on the link to see real-time updates on the status of your delivery and estimated arrival time.

What if there's an issue with my order?

If you encounter any problems with your order, such as missing items or incorrect items, please reach out to our customer support team right away. We're here to help and will do everything we can to make it right.

Can I make changes to my order after it's been placed?

Unfortunately, we're unable to make changes to orders once they've been submitted. However, if you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our support team as soon as possible, and we'll do our best to assist you.
